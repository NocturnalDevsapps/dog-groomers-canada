Editorial Policy

Dog Groomers Canada is built to help people compare grooming options with clear navigation, source transparency, and original booking guidance.

Directory data

Profiles use business listing facts such as name, city, address, phone, website, rating, hours, service signals, and map links when available. We do not publish customer review text.

Original guidance

City, service, and profile pages add practical comparison notes so visitors know what to confirm before booking a grooming appointment.

Corrections

Businesses and visitors can request updates, removals, or corrections by email. Changed details are reviewed before the static site is regenerated.

Advertising and independence

Listings are not endorsements and are not ranked because a business paid for placement. Advertising may appear on the site after review approval, but ads do not change directory facts, city pages, service pages, or correction handling.

To avoid misleading visitors during review, display ad placements are disabled until the site is approved and ready to serve ads.

Images and copyrighted material

Business profiles use a site-owned illustrated placeholder instead of republishing third-party business photos. This keeps the directory focused on factual comparison details and avoids relying on images the directory does not own.
