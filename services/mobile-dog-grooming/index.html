How to compare mobile dog grooming

Mobile grooming is convenient, but availability, parking, water, power, dog size, and service area can affect fit.

Service radius

Confirm that your address is inside the groomer's route and ask about travel fees.

Setup needs

Ask about driveway access, parking space, power or water requirements, and weather limitations.

Dog comfort

Mobile grooming can suit anxious dogs, but confirm noise level, crate use, and owner presence rules.
